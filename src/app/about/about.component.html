Civinomics mission is to refresh democracy for a digital world.

Civinomics was founded in 2011 with the vision that governance of by and for the people could be better than ever in today’s interconnected world. Our non-partisan belief is that the challenges of the modern era are solvable if people come together to share information and develop solutions.

We’ve gone through many iterations of our application and will surely go through many more as we march towards that glorious future. We’ve worked with numerous governments throughout California running online forums, statistically representative outreach surveys, and in person events. We embrace new opportunities and collaborations. Contact us today.

civi - nomics

civil: of the commonwealth or state

nomics: derived from the Greek nomos, meaning law, order, arrangement, systematized knowledge of [something]. The fields ending with -nomics thus mean "systematized knowledge" of whatever the prefix is

civinomics: the systematized knowledge of the commonwealth
